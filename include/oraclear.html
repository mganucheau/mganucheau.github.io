OraClear


Client: Institute for the Future
Role: Technical Lead
Technology Used: Raspberry Pi, Java (Processing)

OraClear is work of speculative design, a physical computing prototype designed to support a forecasting scenario in which an individual’s trust level is assessed by a corporation.

The unit consists of a Raspberry Pi, microphone, battery, touch screen display, 3D printed housing and custom software written in Java.

This prototype was created and used live at the Institute for the Future’s 50th anniversary conference in 2018.
